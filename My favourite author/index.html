GEORGE S. CLASON

Biography

George Samuel Clason was an American Author. He was born on the 7th of November 1874, and died on the 7th of April 1957. He is most often associated with his book The Richest Man In Babylon.


Clason also started two companies, the Clason map Company of Denver, Colorado and the Clason Publishing Company.


Clason is best known for writing a series of informational pamphlets about being thrifty and how to achieve financial success.

Books He Wrote

  1. The Richest Man In Babylon
  2. Gold Ahead
  3. Find your Right Job
  4. Catholic, Jew, Klux Klan: What They Believe, Where They Conflict.
